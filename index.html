Theoretical Background

The Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP) is a combinatorial optimization problem seeking to minimize the total route cost while serving all customers with vehicle capacity constraints.

Minimize: Z = Σᵢⱼ cᵢⱼxᵢⱼ Subject to: Σⱼ xᵢⱼ = 1, ∀i ∈ V\{0} Σᵢ dᵢyᵢₖ ≤ Q, ∀k ∈ K

where:

  • cᵢⱼ: cost of traveling from node i to j
  • xᵢⱼ: binary decision variable
  • dᵢ: demand at node i
  • Q: vehicle capacity
